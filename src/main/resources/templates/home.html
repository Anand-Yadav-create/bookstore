WELCOME TO MY BOOK STORE

Okay, here’s my answer: One: Books. Books, books, books. Not computer screens or docs, real books, with bound pages and spines and words. The smell of books, the feeling of holding books in your hands. Just the fact that you can go to a place and be surrounded by books is crazy wonderful. And the varieties of books, wow, that’s pretty exciting too. The idea that so many different people have had so many different ideas and that they have labored to put those ideas into words and then onto pages is just remarkable. Two: Because you know someone truly unique and amazing and you want to give them a gift that befits them and, what’s a better gift than a book?
